# SUBJECT.evaluate.md

## Intent

Assess support or refutation of a claim

## Reader Values

- Evidence bearing on specific claims
- Strength and limitations of that evidence
- Caveats and alternative interpretations
- Direct relevance to evaluation target
- Honest assessment including weaknesses

## Reader Tolerates

- Narrow focus on evaluation target
- Ignoring findings unrelated to the claim
- Uncertainty in conclusions

## Reader Does Not Want

- Comprehensive summary unrelated to evaluation
- One-sided presentation
- Overlooking contradictory evidence
- False confidence
